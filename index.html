Who can benefit from tutoring?

Tutoring can benefit students who are having difficulty with math, those who are looking for new challenges beyond their classwork, and everyone in between. 

Depending on individual student needs, tutoring sessions can include:

  • Additional support in areas where students are struggling

  • Enrichment for students seeking more advanced work

  • Homework help

  • Test prep

  • Skill maintenance over school breaks (which is especially important in the summer months!)

  • Course content previewing to prepare for the next school year

 

Why Math Tutoring?

Tutoring provides students with one-on-one instruction that is catered to their learning style and individual needs.  

Benefits of tutoring include:

  • Individualized instruction

  • The opportunity to approach topics in a variety of ways

  • Boosted student confidence 

  • A deeper understanding of content

  • Strengthening essential foundational skills

  • Reinforcement and maintenance of skills that have been previously learned

  • Improved work and study habits

  • A more positive attitude towards math
